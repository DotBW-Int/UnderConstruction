Privacy Policy

Introduction

CTriangle Cost Manager ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection & Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We implement industry-standard encryption to protect your information. No personal data is transmitted to external servers.

  • All financial data remains on your device
  • Bank-grade encryption for data protection
  • No cloud storage - complete privacy

App Permissions

  • Storage access (for backup features)
  • Camera (for receipt photos)
  • Biometric authentication (for secure access)

Updates & Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
